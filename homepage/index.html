Welcome to the Sales Calculator

The sales calculator is a tool which you can make use of to come up with markups and margins based on their cost and RRP.
This is a multifunctional tool, in which in total their are 4 different tools.
Each subtitle from the Menu above describes the tool that you will be using, start off by clicking any of the menu options, thereonwards everything is straight forward and easy to use.
